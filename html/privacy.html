The Information SoundKit Collects

When you visit the Site, you provide us with two types of information: personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected by us and Site use information collected by us as you interact with our Site.

When you purchase a product through SoundKit, you provide us with certain personal information, such as your name, your e-mail address, your location, and any other personal or preference information that you choose to share with us.

When you enter the Site, SoundKit collects your browser type and IP address. This information is gathered for all Site visitors. In addition, SoundKit stores certain information from your browser using "cookies." A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. SoundKit uses session ID cookies for several purposes, including to confirm that users are logged in. SoundKit uses a persistent cookie that stores information to make it easier for you to login when you come back to the Site. You can remove or block this cookie using the settings in your browser if you want to disable this convenience feature.

Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on the Site is a violation of the SoundKit Terms of Service and should be reported by using our contact form to send us an e-mail.

By using the Site, you are consenting to have your personal data transferred to and processed in the United States.

If you are under 13, please do not attempt to purchase SoundKit products or send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address. No one under age 13 may provide any personal information to or on the Site. In the event that SoundKit learns that SoundKit has collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, SoundKit will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that SoundKit might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us by using our contact form to send us an e-mail.
